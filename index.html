Projects

EcoTwin AI – Digital Twin for Urban Sustainability Planning

Python, XGBoost, Random Forest, BERT, PostgreSQL–PostGIS, FastAPI, CesiumJS

• Built a city-scale AI-driven urban digital twin integrating satellite remote sensing, geospatial machine learning, and transformer-based NLP, deployed via a cloud-native architecture with interactive 3D visualization and PDF-based simulation reports covering feasibility, energy efficiency, cost, and pollution impact.

• Implemented an integrated urban intelligence pipeline combining XGBoost-based Urban Heat Island modeling (R² = 0.685, MAE = 1.34°C), a Random Forest–based sustainable material recommendation engine (93% accuracy, 87% with user preferences), and BERT-based geo-semantic sentiment analysis using web-scraped Reddit posts and news articles across 225 wards in Bengaluru.

• Authored a research paper titled “EcoTwin AI: Digital Twin for Urban Sustainability Planning”, accepted at the International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Control Systems (ICICCS-2026).

Hybrid Network Intrusion Detection System

Python, Scikit-learn, Random Forest, Isolation Forest, Autoencoders

• Designed and implemented a hybrid Intrusion Detection System integrating supervised, unsupervised, and reconstruction-based learning with a confidence-based adaptive ensemble strategy to detect both known and zero-day attacks.

• Evaluated on the CICIDS2017 dataset, achieving 99.55% accuracy, 1.00 precision, and ROC-AUC of 0.9898; built a user interface for real-time CSV uploads, automated risk classification, and anomaly insights.

• Authored a research paper titled “An Adaptive Hybrid Ensemble Framework for Network Intrusion Detection Using Supervised, Unsupervised and Deep Reconstruction Learning,” presented at the 9th IEEE International Conference on Computational Systems and Information Technology for Sustainable Solutions (CSITSS-2025), published in IEEE Xplore.
